How Console Games Try To Addict You To Them


By Walker Salmon

Endless play

One Tactic used by some Video Games is Endless Game Play. This tactic preys on the fact that the human brain recognizes beginnings and ends. Our brains recognize an ending and are more likely to move on to another activity once one activity is completed Additionally if we are interuppted during one activity we are far more likely to return to that activity. Most Open World video games such as Skyrim and Fallout do not have an 'end' at which the story is finished, it is objective after objective in a near endless stream of entertainment. This makes it difficult to put down games like these and increase the chance of returning to the game later.

Loot boxes

A loot box is a in game prize which gives the player a chance at receiving in-game items and cosmetics. This type of random intermittent reward system provides nearly identical stimulation to the brain as a casino slot machine would. This slot-machine-like reward system has been banned in certain countries and has caused certain gaming corporations to recieve lawsuits for violating online gambling laws. Certain companies have even admitted to using predatory tactics to direct players towards purchasing these lootboxes. This type of intermittent rewards system is one of the most addictive things in the world.

Ideas to help you

Simply put, power off the console, find something you can do with your time that will make you feel better. some good examples are
  • Play console games for a limited time.
  • Go for a walk outside
  • Take a nap
  • Go to the Library
  • Pick up a new hobby
  • Exersise
