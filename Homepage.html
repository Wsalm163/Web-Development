How Your Technology Addicts You To It


By Walker Salmon

Our mission

Technology is a wonderful and fantastic tool for us all to enjoy. We have near infinite amounts of information and entertainment at our fingertips and in our pockets. However we need to make sure that we are using these tools correctly. Sadly some forms of electronic use are designed to addict its users. This addiction is ubiquitous in our society and causes the users to feel less joy in even their favourite activities. Our objective is to educate and inform people about these problems and help people to learn how to use their devices in a healthy manner.

How much is too much?

In our daily lives we look at devices and screens constantly. So what is the proper amount of screen use? Experts recommend that children under 2 years of age should not have recreational screen time. For children aged 2 to 5 years, recreational screen time should be limited to 1 hour per day. Everyone older than 5 years should limit their daily recreational screen time to 2 hours.
Quick Reference Table
Age Group Recommended screen time limit
Under 2 years No screen time
2-5 years 1 hour daily
>5 years 2 hours daily

What counts as recreational screen time?

Recreational screen time can be defined many ways, some common recreational uses of screen time are:
  • Watching streaming services such as Netflix or Disney+
  • Using social media in any form
  • Playing video games on a gaming console, computer or smartphone
  • Scrolling endlessly through videos on a personal device
